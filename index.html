About Us

At SAW Recruitment, we aim to revolutionize the staffing industry by creating meaningful connections between talent and leading companies. Our dedicated team strives to deliver exceptional service and results. According to India’s Ministry of External Affairs, 215,270 international students moved to Canada last year, with 65% of them holding existing credentials and actively seeking employment. We are committed to serving the South Asian community by connecting talent with employers through our platform. Our Founder, Danny Sivarajah, and Co-Founder, Thanuya Mohanathas, bring a wealth of experience and expertise in public policy, recruitment, change management, consulting, training and development, real estate, hospitality and tourism, technology, healthcare, and the construction industry. Their diverse backgrounds provide an in-depth understanding of the needs and challenges facing both job seekers and employers in today’s unique and competitive market.

Mission

To empower South Asian workers by granting them with meaningful employment opportunities, fostering their personal and professional growth, and advocating for their rights and wel-being in the workplace.

Vision

"We envision a future where every South Asian worker feels valued, respected, and empowered in their employment, leading to stronger communities and a more equitable and inclusive society."
